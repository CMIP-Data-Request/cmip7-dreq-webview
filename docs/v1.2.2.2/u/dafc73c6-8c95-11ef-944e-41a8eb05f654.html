Category Description

These are non-exclusive grouping of CMIP variables (e.g. monthly time slices of the baseline variables). Variables can belong to more than one group.



AttributeValue
justificationThis variable group is requesting a range of 2D variables at sub-daily timescales. Examples include variables at the surface that are relevant to air quality (e.g, ozone, particulate matter with a diameter less than 2.5 um, etc. ); Other variables are requested to understand aerosol extinction and backscatter, as well as variables to analyse climate responses due to changes in short-lived climate forcers (e.g, surface temperature).
