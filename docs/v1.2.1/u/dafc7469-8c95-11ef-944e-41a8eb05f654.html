Category Description

These are non-exclusive grouping of CMIP variables (e.g. monthly time slices of the baseline variables). Variables can belong to more than one group.



AttributeValue
justificationThis is a request for monthly resolution variables necessary to analyze the evolution of the sea ice energy budget and understand the drivers of sea ice mass tendencies, thus satisfying the requirements of the "Sea ice changes, drivers and impacts" opportunity.
nameseaice_budget_energy_monthly
notesThis variable group includes heat fluxes relevant to sea ice both from the sea ice and the atmospheric components. This is because, depending on the model, the full decomposition of the different fluxes might be available only in one component or the others, so to make sure all the terms to close the sea ice energy budget are present we request them in both components.
titleVariables needed for assessing the sea ice energy budget at monthly timeslices
