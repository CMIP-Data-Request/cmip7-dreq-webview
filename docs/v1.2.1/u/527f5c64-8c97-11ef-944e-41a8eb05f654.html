Model Intercomparison Project



AttributeValue
mip_abstractA better understanding of the role of sea ice for the changing climate of our planet is the central aim of the diagnostic Sea-Ice Model Intercomparison Project (SIMIP), having formally started for CMIP6. To reach this aim, SIMIP requests sea-ice-related variables from climate-model simulations that allow for a better understanding and, ultimately, improvement of biases and errors in sea-ice simulations with large-scale climate models. This then allows us to better understand to what degree CMIP model simulations relate to reality, thus improving our confidence in answering sea-ice-related questions based on these simulations. Furthermore, the SIMIP protocol provides a standard for sea-ice model output that will streamline and hence simplify the analysis of the simulated sea-ice evolution in research projects independent of CMIP. To reach its aims, SIMIP provides a structured list of model output that allows for an examination of the evolution of sea ice.
