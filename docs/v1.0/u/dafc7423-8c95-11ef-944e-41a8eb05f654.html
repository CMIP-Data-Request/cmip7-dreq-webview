AttributeValue
justificationThe 3-hourly atmosphere-ocean surface fluxes (freshwater, heat, and momentum) are required input, that will allow for a bias-adjustment of the oceanic forcings provided within ISIMIP. So far the oceanic forcing is not bias-adjusted although that is quite critical for the regional marine ecosystem and fisheries models that are calibrated by observational data. For a test case (sensitivity experiment in ISIMIP3b) we have developed a method to bypass the need for the fluxes. However, that is restricted to a specific ESM. The 3-hourly fluxes will allow for a more straight forward bias-adjustment of other ESMs, too. The approach is described in Lengaigne et al. (2024) (https://essopenarchive.org/users/717160/articles/1151003-an-ocean-only-framework-for-correcting-future-cmip-oceanic-projections-from-their-present-day-biases) and also described in the ISIMIP3b protocol paper (Frieler et al., 2024, to be submitted and available on request). Variables mandatory for bias adjustment: 3h.tauuo, 3h.tauvo, 3h.hfds, 3h.rsntds, 3h.friver, 3h.wfo Additional variables to close budget but not mandatory: 3h.ficeberg, 3h.flandice, 3h.sfdsi, 3h.hfrunoffds
