cf_standard_namemass_content_of_water_in_soil_layer_defined_by_root_depth
description"Content" indicates a quantity per unit area. The content of a soil layer is the vertical integral of the specified quantity within the layer. The quantity with standard name mass\_content\_of\_water\_in\_soil\_layer\_defined\_by\_root\_depth is the vertical integral between the surface and the depth to which plant roots penetrate. A coordinate variable or scalar coordinate variable with standard name root\_depth can be used to specify the extent of the layer. "Water" means water in all phases.
