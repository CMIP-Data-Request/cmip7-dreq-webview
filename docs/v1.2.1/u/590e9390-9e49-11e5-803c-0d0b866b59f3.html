descriptionComputed strength of the ice pack, defined as the energy (J m-2) dissipated per unit area removed from the ice pack under compression, and assumed proportional to the change in potential energy caused by ridging. For Hibler-type models, this is P = P\* h exp(-C(1-A)) where P\* is compressive strength, h is ice thickness, A is compactness and C is strength reduction constant.
