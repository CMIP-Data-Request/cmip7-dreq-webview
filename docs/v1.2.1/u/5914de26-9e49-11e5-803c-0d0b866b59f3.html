description"Content" indicates a quantity per unit area. "Wood debris" means dead organic matter composed of coarse wood. It is distinct from fine litter. The sum of the quantities with standard names wood\_debris\_mass\_content\_of\_nitrogen, surface\_litter\_mass\_content\_of\_nitrogen and subsurface\_litter\_mass\_content\_of\_nitrogen is the total nitrogen mass content of dead plant material.
