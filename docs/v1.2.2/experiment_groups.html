Category: experiment_groups (v1.2.2)

Back to Category Index

    Category Description: These are non-exclusive grouping of experiments (e.g. ‘Fast Track’, ‘DECK’, ‘Scenarios’ etc.). Experiments can belong to more than one group.

  • aerchemmip (All AerChemMIP simulations)
  • all-non-fasttrack
  • cfmip-additional-nonfasttrack (Additional CFMIP CMIP7 experiments not in Fast Track)
  • cfmip_amip_amip4K (AMIP and AMIP+4K experiments for intensive CFMIP analysis)
  • climate_extreme (Climate extreme analysis additional experiments)
  • conc-driven-scenario-extensions (Concentration-driven CMIP7 scenario extensions experiments)
  • damip_experiment_group (All experiments relevant to the DAMIP opportunity)
  • dcpp (All DCPP CMIP7 experiments)
  • dcpp-cmip6-exps (DCPP Fast Track predictions initialized in 2025 or 2026 will require CMIP6 hindcasts as a pre-requisite. This has been discussed with the CMIP panel and the WIP. This Experiment group contains the relevant experiments to enable this.)
  • deck
  • deck_idealized_co2_experiments (Idealized co2 deck experiments)
  • dynvar (The group of experiments of highest interest for DynVarMIP)
  • fast-track (Experiments included in the CMIP Assessment Fast Track (not inclusive of DECK experiments))
  • fast-track_cfmip (CFMIP subset of FastTrack)
  • firemip-nonfasttrack (FireMIP experiments for CMIP7 (experiments are not in the Fast Track))
  • firemip_deck_subset (Subset of deck experiments for FireMIP opportunity)
  • firemip_fast-track_subset (Subset of experiments from fast-track to support fire assessment)
  • highresmip2-ia (Subset of highresmip2 experiments)
  • historical
  • impact_climserv_core_expt (Core experiments needed for climate service provision)
  • irrmip-nonfasttrack (IRRMIP experiments)
  • ismip7-scenario-extensions (Scenario extension experiments needed for ISMIP7)
  • lmip (Land-only historical and scenario simulations)
  • picontrol
  • pmip (Paleoclimate experiment)
  • scenarios
  • scenarios_extensions (Experiment group for the extension scenario experiments)
  • scenarios_extensions-low-medium-high (Just the low, medium, and high scenarios extensions)
  • synoptic_systems_and_impacts (This an experiment group that accompanies the synoptic_systems_and_impact opportunity)
  • temperature_variability_opportunity (The experiment group that aligns with the proposed temperature variability opportunity.)
  • tipmip-experiments
