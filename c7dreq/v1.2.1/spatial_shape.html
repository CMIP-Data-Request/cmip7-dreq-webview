Category: spatial_shape (v1.2.1)

Back to Category Index

    Category Description: The spatial shape record contains the spatial dimensions of the field, and also, for convenience, an integer specifying the number of levels if that number is specified. A boolean level flag is set to “true” if the number of vertical levels is specified.

  • GYB-R (Ocean basin meridional section (on model grid and density surfaces))
  • S-A (Atmospheric profiles at specified sites)
  • S-AH (Atmospheric profiles (half levels) at specified sites)
  • S-na (Site (specific locations requested by CFMIP))
  • TR-na (Ocean transect)
  • TRS-na (Ocean transect for sea-ice fluxes)
  • XY-A (Global field on model atmosphere levels)
  • XY-AH (Global field on model atmosphere half-levels)
  • XY-B (Global ocean field on layers)
  • XY-H16 (Global field (16 altitudes))
  • XY-H40 (Global field on 40 altitude levels)
  • XY-O (Global ocean field on model levels)
  • XY-P19 (Global field (19 pressure levels))
  • XY-P3 (Global field (3 pressure levels))
  • XY-P5u (Global field (5 pressure levels, UTLS))
  • XY-P6 (Global field (6 pressure levels))
  • XY-P7 (Global field (7 pressure levels))
  • XY-P7T (Global field (7 pressure tropospheric levels))
  • XY-S (Global field on soil levels)
  • XY-SF (Global field at sea floor)
  • XY-na (Global field (single level))
  • XYA-na (Polar stereographic grid (Antarctic))
  • XYG-na (Polar stereographic grid (Greenland))
  • Y-P39 (Atmospheric zonal mean (on 39 pressure levels))
  • Y-na (Zonal mean (on surface))
